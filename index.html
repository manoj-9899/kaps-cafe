OUR STORY

Born in Mahad.
Made for everyone.

Kaps Café started with one simple idea — great food should not cost a fortune. Located in the heart of Mahad, we serve the town's favourite burgers, pizzas, momos, shawarma, and so much more.

Whether you are here for a quick bite after college, a family outing on the weekend, or just a cold coffee on a warm afternoon — there is always a seat waiting for you at Kaps.

Every item on our menu is made fresh, served hot, and priced so everyone can enjoy it. That has always been our promise to Mahad.
